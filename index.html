Our Services

Laundry Card

You don't have to worry about getting exact change to the store as we provide rechargeable laundry cards. You can use cash or credit cards to add money to your card.

Multi-Size Washers & Dryers

We have plenty of options when it comes to choosing washers and dryers for loads of varying sizes. We make sure that you don't spend more money than is needed to wash and dry your clothes.

Forgot Soap? No worries!

We have our own soap dispenser that enables you to buy top quality detergent should you forget or choose not to bring your own.

Vending Machine

We have plenty of waiting space with LCD TV(s) to entertain you while your clothes are drying. We even have a vending machine just in case you crave a snack.
